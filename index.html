ABOUT

Based in NYC, Ximena has had a passion for the arts since her youth expirementing in instruments and art. After soon dropping the trumpet she began to dedicate herself to painting and drawing inspired by the renisance painters that came before her she specializes in traditional oil painting methods and drawing methods Below is a list of art mediums she specializes in as well as preparatory grounds for works she is able to do

  1. Oil paint
  2. Graphite pencil
  3. Charcoal
  4. Watercolor
  5. Acrylic
    • Hand preppared paper for drawings
    • Hand prepared oil and acrylic ground

Please note all oil paintings on linen have been hand stretched and prepared with handmade oil ground.
